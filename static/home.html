Some Information

This site is hosted on my local NixOS k3s cluster at my residence. It employs Fastify for serving and ngrok as a reverse proxy. Additionally, several other applications operate on different ports. The primary objectives of this website are:
1. To showcase my projects and code to the public and potential interviewers.
2. To expose my services, allowing my applications to access my k3s cluster.
You may notice the api.felixhub.dev subdomain; this includes endpoints for various services running on my k3s cluster. If you attempt to utilize them without authorization, you will be redirected to the login page, as access requires a special key. If you wish to access these services, please contact me.
